About

Brent Dixon founded Dixon Strategic Labs in 2022 to guide leaders through strategic crossroads. He works with credit unions and community financial institutions across North America to align teams, chart growth paths, and ship results.

He has built and led programs, prototypes, and pilots with Filene Research Institute, the United Nations, and Singularity University.

He lives in Oakland, California with his son and a bossy cat named Bug.
